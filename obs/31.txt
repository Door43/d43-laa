====== Si Jesus Mipanaw tu Dibabaw Thubig ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-01.jpg}}

Mandyare, si Jesus tinaluanen su nga disciples en nga sumakay ilan na tu gbelutu bu medlayag ilan na tu selebaloy danaw dahag pempuli en na su nga getaw. Metubos puliay ni Jesus su nga getaw, minangay hyanin tu rapit gbentud buwid para meningi-nengi. Sala nen lak ritu, peningi-nengi Hyanin sampay hapit na gmedali gendaw.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-02.jpg}}

Sama si Jesus ritu pa gbentud buwid, su nga tinintuluan nen phemelula na tu gbelutu, mesempel na dali gendaw mirateng ilan na tu dlawd danaw. Melasay ilan thanding tu hephemelula lan haguna meseheg thanding su sambel mesumbak lan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-03.jpg}}

Muha si Jesus metubos na meningi-ningi dahag menangay na hyanin tu nga tinintuluan nen. Mepanaw Hyanin tu dibabaw thubig pegangay rin gbelutu lan!



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-04.jpg}}

Su nga tinintuluan nen mendek ilan thanding mba minita lan si Jesus,  haguna rin pihilan lan mikita ilan khalag. Mesunan ni Jesus nga mendek ilan, bina tinawag ilan ni Jesus bu methalu, "Gendi a mu hendek. Inan nini!"



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-05.jpg}}

Muha methalu si Peter tu ni Jesus, "Kunutan, mba yaa en, sugu mu inan nga mangay u rin Ryania rin dibabaw thubig." Dahag sumimbag si Jesus, "Ali a!"



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-06.jpg}}

Bina, guminawas si Peter ritu gbelutu dahag mesugod na hyanin panaw rin dibabaw thubig pangay tu ni Jesus. Ugaid sa mekapanaw na hyanin tu thubig, ginawa en su hetinggi en tu ni Jesus muha phetenggi nen su nga balod dahus mebatek en su sambel meseheg.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-07.jpg}}

Bina, mendek si Peter bu melened hyanin tu thubig. Megayad hyanin, "Kunutan, gun mu inan!" Tibangan hyanin ni Jesus muha ginawid rin hyanin. Muha tinaluan nen si Peter, " Yaa hin getaw mekaka hin hesaleg en, masa medwa-dwaa?"



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-08.jpg}}

When Peter and Jesus got into the boat, the wind immediately stopped blowing and the water became calm. The disciples were amazed. They worshiped Jesus, saying to him, “Truly, you are the Son of God.”



// A Bible story from: Matthew 14:22-33; Mark 6:45-52; John 6:16-21 //
