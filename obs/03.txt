====== Su gembagel baah ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-01.jpg}}

Minaya su melon ton, midlehelun su nga getaw pegbenwa ri dunya. Sugat na i hebalarusa lan bu sugat i hebelu lan.  Mebal ilan na melat bu su Diwata mepemehelin nga gebaenen su dliwag dunya ri gembagel baah.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-02.jpg}}

Ugaid su Diwata lelyag tu hetubo ni Noah. Hyanin mitilak su hetubuen, pya pegbenwa ritu nga getaw balarusa. Su Diwata theluanen si Noah mahasud tu baah nga gentangan nu Diwata pwetay. Theluan nen si Noah nga megbal gebagel beluto.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-03.jpg}}

Su Diwata theluanen si Noah mahatag tu beluto balenen, megatos patpolu metros dyabaen bu dwapulo butlo metros i beklangen dahus i tasen sepulo butlo bu gininga metros. Si Noah balenen heni di bebalan gayo bu megbal telu panghat, melon suguren, gatepen    bu selabuk palinluwanganen. Su beluto henenin su gumon ni Noah bu tu nga pamilya nen, dahus tu helun  petubuen ri dlupaan segay rin baah.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-04.jpg}}

Si Noah mengunut tu Diwata. Hyanin bu su thelu bataen dlai megbal rin beluto nga thenalu nu Diwata ryanlan. Meratengan melon ton su hepegbal rin beluto, haguna sugat hebagelen. Si Noah memalinta tunga getaw nga dun gembagel baah merateng bu theluanen su nga getaw nga puli ilan tu Diwata, ugaid genda ilan pephetud ryanin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-05.jpg}}

Su Diwata sinuguen si Noah bu su pamilya nen nga metigum gan para rin ryanlan bu tu nga petubuen. Ba su helunen mesagepa na, su Diwata theluanen si Noah, su sawanen,bu su telu bataen dlai bu su nga  tilu sawa nu nga bataen nga sumeled ilan tu beluto gwalo getaw su dlanen mehashelid tu beluto.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-06.jpg}}

Su Diwata mepwet nga dlai bu bahalan tu helon nga pitubuen bu nga manok- manok tu ni Noah para peseleren to belotu ba su dyangha nu merateng na su baah meluwas ilan. Su Diwata mepwet rema nga pitubok dlai bu pitubok nga bahalan nga dlen-dlen nga pitubuen nga megamit lan  ri hepedulang.  Ba sama kitu na dlunan lan tu belotu, su Diwata  penintu en su pinto.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-07.jpg}}

Su dupi mepun na pedupi, pedupi, bu pedupi.  Pedupi sheled rin patpulo daw muha patpulo gebi da pakening! Su tubig genat di dlupa luminyawa sugat hebagelen. Su dlunen tu dlewag dunya metebunan na ri tubig, pya su metas thanding bentud.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-08.jpg}}

Su helunen nga pegbenwa ri dlupaan mimatay,su lak nga getaw  bu su nga pitubuen nga kitu beluto genda matay. Su beluto pedlentaw-lentaw tu tubig  bu su helunen nga kitu sheled beluto miguwn ilan genat ri helened.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-09.jpg}}

Ba su dupi migbeneng na, su beluto peg dlentaw-lentaw ri tubig sheled rin lima bulan, su dyangha hya su tubig mepun na mityan. Selabuk gendaw su beluto mebara tu dibabaw bentud, ugaid su dunya metebunan pa tubig. Shelid rin thelu bulan, su dibabaw nu nga gbentud minita na.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-10.jpg}}

After forty more days, Noah sent out a bird called a raven to see if the water had dried up. The raven flew back and forth looking for dry land, but it could not find any.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-11.jpg}}

Later Noah sent out a bird called a dove. But it also could not find any dry land, so it came back to Noah. A week later he sent the dove out again, and it came back with an olive branch in its beak! The water was going down, and the plants were growing again!



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-12.jpg}}

Noah waited another week and sent out the dove a third time. This time, it found a place to rest and did not come back. The water was drying up!



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-13.jpg}}

Two months later God said to Noah, “You and your family and all the animals may leave the boat now. Have many children and grandchildren and fill the earth.” So Noah and his family came out of the boat.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-14.jpg}}

After Noah got off the boat, he built an altar and sacrificed some of each kind of animal that could be used for a sacrifice. God was happy with the sacrifice and blessed Noah and his family.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-15.jpg}}

God said, “I promise I will never again curse the ground because of the evil things that people do, or destroy the world by causing a flood, even though people are sinful from the time they are children.”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-16.jpg}}

God then made the first rainbow as a sign of his promise. Every time the rainbow appeared in the sky, God would remember what he promised and so would his people.



// A Bible story from: Genesis 6-8 //
