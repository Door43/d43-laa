====== Si Moses Tinawag nu Diwata ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-01.jpg}}

Sama minatay na si Joseph, su helunan parintenen pegbenwa ritu Egypt. Yanlan bu su nga thupwanen peperayun helan pegbinwa ra ritu, melon ton muha medlehelon na su nga bata lan. Bina tinawag elan Israelites.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-02.jpg}}

Menya  su megatus ton, medlehelon na tandeng su nga Israelite. Hitu nga Egyptian da ran na meremdimay  si Joseph dahus hitu dlunan nga megabangen rin ryanlan. Mendek hilan tunga Israelites kay pekhelon  hilan na tandeng, si Pharaoh nga pedlegari ritu Egypt,  binalen so nga Israelites nga gulepen ritu nga Egyptians.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-03.jpg}}

Su nga Egyptian pemegis  ran su nga Israelites megbal melon mahabagel balay pya ritu dlewag syudad. Su hetegel ran megbal megrabi su helasay tu hitobo lan, ugaid su Diwata pengumpyananen elan bu  mekelon pa su nga bata lan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-04.jpg}}

Menita ni Pharaoh su nga Israelites melon na tandeg su nga bata lan, senuguen su nga getawen nga pitayenlan su nga dlunan gembata dlae nga Israelites, bu delahen ran ritu sapa Nile.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-05.jpg}}

Selabok dlebun Israelite mehabata dlai. Hyanin bu hitu sawa nen gedlud lan su gembata sampay mekaya lan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-06.jpg}}

Sa gendi lan na mepegidlud su gembata su gina en bu gama en betang lan su gembata rin delus (basket), metubus pyanud lan ritu sapa Nile para megown su gembata ren hepatay. Hitu phated rin dlebun petinggenen ra alan me pegbetad tu gembata.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-07.jpg}}

Menita nu gbata dlebun ni Pharaoh hitu delus muha petinggenen alandun shelerin tu delus. Sama menita en na su gembata, benalen na hyanin bataen. Minalap hyanen Israelites dlebun para megengat ritu gembata. Daen mesunay nga su Israelite dlebun hitu, hin ra su gina nu gembata. Sama mebagel na su gembata, genda en na kinahanglay su gatas rin ginaen, pepuli en na su gembata ritu gbata dlibun ni Pharaoh muha pengengalananen si Moses.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-08.jpg}}

Selabuk gendaw, ba si Moses mebagel na, minita en su Egyptian nga peg bentulenen su gulepen  Israelite. Si Moses pikindananen luwasay su sama nen Israelite.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-09.jpg}}

Lungdon ni Moses daen mehita ryanin, penatayen hitu Egyptian dahag lebengen su dlawasen. Ugaid dun mehita ritu benal ni Moses.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-10.jpg}}

When Pharaoh heard what Moses had done, he tried to kill him. Moses ran away from Egypt to the wilderness where he would be safe from Pharaoh’s soldiers.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-11.jpg}}

Moses became a shepherd in the wilderness far away from Egypt. He married a woman from that place and had two sons.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-12.jpg}}

One day while Moses was taking care of his sheep, he saw a bush that was on fire. But the bush did not burn up. Moses went towards the bush to be able to see it better. As he approached the burning bush, the voice of God said, “Moses, take off your shoes. You are standing on holy ground.”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-13.jpg}}

God said, “I have seen the suffering of my people. I will send you to Pharaoh so that you can bring the Israelites out of their slavery in Egypt. I will give them the land of Canaan, the land I promised to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-14.jpg}}

Moses asked, “What if the people want to know who sent me, what should I say?” God said, “I AM WHO I AM. Tell them, ‘I AM has sent me to you.’ Also tell them, ‘I am Yahweh, the God of your ancestors Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.’ This is my name forever.”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-15.jpg}}

Moses was afraid and did not want to go to Pharaoh because he thought he could not speak well, so God sent Moses’ brother, Aaron, to help him. God warned Moses and Aaron that Pharaoh would be stubborn.



// A Bible story from: Exodus 1-4 //
