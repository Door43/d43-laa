====== si John gi Baptisanen si Jesus ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-01.jpg}}

Si John, bata ninga Zechariah bu ni Elizabeth,  me bagel hyanin nandaw bu mibal prophet. Peg benwa hyanin di gulangan e pekanen tineb bu gapanapan, bu panit e dlahu en nga genat ri panit ri camel.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-02.jpg}}

Melon getaw mangay  tu gulangan nga mekeneg  tu ni John. Su pegwali nen, mitalu,wa ay nyo su dusa nyo, kay su pidlegerian nu Diwta misepel na.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-03.jpg}}

Sama su nga getaw merngeg lan su nga tintuluan ni John melon rin ryan lan nga tilyuran lan su nga su dusa lan, bu si John  ge baptisan yan lan. Melon ra rema nga kunutan ri simbahan mingay para baptisan ni John, ugaid inda lan tilyuray su nga dusa lan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-04.jpg}}

Si John metalu tunga kunutan nunga di Simbahan, Yamu nga bisa nipay, tilyu ray nyo su nga dusa nyo bu pegbugu amo na tunga gulula nyo nga gulola nyo nga di mahan gito. Ba sama  su gayo nga endi mimunga misaba peren yanin bu dilahen tun gapoy, Si John me pegbetaren lan su tinalo nunga prophet. Tengi amo pewet u nga meguna nga metalu tu guheten  mo, nga hyanin su mi sagipa tu dalan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-05.jpg}}

sabad nga nga jew misak nga marun hyanin ba su Gumon  si John sumimbag, hena inan su Gumon, ugaid dun selabuk ngamerateng nga sunod ryanan. di gani hyanin melepeng saba hetasen pay su sandalen di mikaya he pelwasurn.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-06.jpg}}

sunud gendaw, Si Jesus minanga rito ni Jonh para baptisan, sama minita hyanin ni John, metalu hynin, tengi nyo su bata dalei bilibili nu Diwata nga waenen su dusa nu dunya.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-07.jpg}}

Si John metalu tuni Jesus, hena patot nga  baptisan ha.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-08.jpg}}

When Jesus came up out of the water after being baptized, the Spirit of God appeared in the form of a dove and came down and rested on him. At the same time, God’s voice spoke from heaven, saying, “You are my Son whom I love, and I am very pleased with you.”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-09.jpg}}

God had told John, “The Holy Spirit will come down and rest on someone you baptize. That person is the Son of God.” There is only one God. But when John baptized Jesus, he heard God the Father speak, saw God the Son, who is Jesus, and he saw the Holy Spirit.



// Matthew 3; Mark 1:9-11; Luke 3:1-23 //
