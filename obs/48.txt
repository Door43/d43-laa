====== Si Jesus Hyanin so Pasad nga Gumon Genesis 1-3,6,14,22; Exodus 12,20; 2 Samuel 7; Hebrew 3:1-6, 4: 14-5:10, 7:1-8;13, 9:11-10:18; Revelation 21 ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-01.jpg}}

Ba benal nu Diwata su dunya, dlunanen melengas. Daen pa dusa. Mekirilamay  si Adam muha Eva, dahus dlelamen lan ra rema su Diwata muha dain pa meset bu hepatay. Hini su lyagan nu Diwata rini dunya.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-02.jpg}}

Su saitan, medlegelun rin ni Eva pebyan rin nepay, ritu garden. Dahag si Eva muha si Adam merusa ilan ditu gunan nu Diwata. Pebyan ritu nga dusa ran su dlunanen metunan meset muha mematay.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-03.jpg}}

Ba si Eva bu si Adam merusa ilan din mebigbitad ran,( melon pa  hin melatin  mepegbitad.) Gbina mebaliran  nguso nu Diwata. Hin su pepunayen umasa ginat khepidlegitaw rin getaw duna  dusa nen.Dahus su mesheba relasyun  lan nu Diwata muha nu getaw mebitangan gilet saba tu dusa. Su Diwata dun planu nen nga mipuli  pa su hasheba relasyun lan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-04.jpg}}

Pinasad nu Diwata tu nga tupwan ni Eva indigan lan su gulo nu saitan, muha  su saitan buten lak su geshelin. mehasud rin nini nga su saitan bunoenen su Gumon. Ugaid su Diwata petubuenen puli su Gumon dahag su gumon gebaenen  su balahat nu saitan sampay di hyanin mehagaga. Mehabayan melun ton, su Diwata memitwa nga si Jesus Hyanin su Gumon.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-05.jpg}}

Ba su Diwata mepwiten su sasat ri dlewag dunya pebya rin baah, Hyanin  ra rema megbegay barko para  meguwn su nga getawen  nga mepitud rin Ryanin. Maantu  ra  rema  tu helonen kay su sebad merusa patut pedlatan kay mi pun  rin dusa lan, ugaid su Diwata phewiten  si JESUS para gumon tu dlun  mepetud rin Ryanin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-06.jpg}}

Shelid rin gatusan ton, su nga pari  peperayun hilan gehapun peg dulang para mampun su nga dusa para petaen lan nga ilan  patut pedlatan nu Diwata aguna balarusa ilan. Ugaid su he dulang nu nga petubuen gendi  mahapapas tu nga dusa lan. Si Jesus Hyanin su metas pari  genda Hyanin mesama ritu helunen. Bigayen su Dyanin hetubu para mebal dulang  sapap  Hyanin su metidu dulang. Su dlunan dusa nga mepegbetad nu nga getaw mampun.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-07.jpg}}

Su Diwata theluan nin si Abraham,"Su dlunan getaw ri dlewag benwa mepengumpyanan pun ryania." Si JESUS Hyanin so thupwan ni Abraham dlunan getaw pegumpyanan phebya ryanin, hagina su dlunanin mephetud ni Jesus, yanin megawn genat rin dusa, dahus tupwan na rema ni Abraham.


Niote:(ispritual descendants)



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-08.jpg}}

Sapap tinalu nu Diwata ri ni Abraham nga balenen dulang si Isaac, ugaid su Diwata mepherun bilibili para mebal dulang imbes tu bata en na si Isaac. Su dlunanen patut  matay sapap rin dusa nen. Ugaid su Diwata bigayen si Jesus,su Metidu bili-bili nu Diwata, para Hyanin su dulang nga matay sukli rin ryanta.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-09.jpg}}

Hitu hapebya nu Diwata nga sasat ritu Egypt,  thiluanen su nga pamilya nu nga Istraelites nga metiti ilan  metaburo bata bili-bili, dahus betangan lan dugo su debabaw nu pintu lan. Haguna, mba mita nu Diwata su dugo, lyusan lak su balay lan, dahus di petayen su nga metau bata lan  dlai. Bhena hini su gulek thenalu nga " SU HELYUS".



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-10.jpg}}

Jesus is our Passover Lamb. He was perfect and sinless and was killed at the time of the Passover celebration. When anyone believes in Jesus, the blood of Jesus pays for that person’s sin, and God’s punishment passes over that person.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-11.jpg}}

God made a covenant with the Israelites, who were his chosen people. But God has now made a New Covenant that is available to everyone. Because of this New Covenant, anyone from any people group can become part of God’s people by believing in Jesus.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-12.jpg}}

Moses was a great prophet who proclaimed the word of God. But Jesus is the greatest prophet of all. He is God, so everything he did and said were the actions and words of God. That is why Jesus is called the Word of God.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-13.jpg}}

God promised King David that one of his descendants would rule as king over God’s people forever. Because Jesus is the Son of God and the Messiah, he is that special descendant of David who can rule forever.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-14.jpg}}

David was the king of Israel, but Jesus is the king of the entire universe! He will come again and rule his kingdom with justice and peace, forever.
