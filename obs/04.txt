====== Su samaya nu Diwata rini Abraham ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-01.jpg}}

Minyan su melon  ton genat tu he baah, su nga getaw mekelon nasap reni dunya, dahag su nga talu lan mesamasama. embis pepenuen lan  su dlewag dunya  kay hin su sinugo nu Diwata ryanlan, mi selabuk ilan hinuun dahag mebal ilan syudad.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-02.jpg}}

Minghabog ilan na dahag inda la na talimaay su Diwata bu su tinaluen. Megbal ilan metas tower nga merepet tu dlangit. Su Diwata minitaen  ba miperayun hilan meselabuk ri gulek ran melat, mekhelun pa gupya su nga dusa lan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-03.jpg}}

Dahag su Diwata pinalin nin su nga thalu lan nga dlen-dlen nga thalu dahag su nga getaw misegwag ilan rin dliwag dunya. Dahag su syudad binal lan tinawag eni  Babel mahasuren  "mahadlibog."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-04.jpg}}

Minayan megatus ton, su Diwata mipinda tu getaw ngalenen si Abram. Tiluan yanin nu Diwata, "awaay mu benwa mu bu nga pamilya mu angay ah tu dlupa nga pitaen hu reni a. pengumpyanan hu yaa muha balen hu yaa gebagel benwa. Pengupyanan hu su mengupya rin reni ah. Balen hu bantugan i ngalan mu. Pengumpyanan hu su mengumpya rin dyania. Busungen hu su mengembusung ren ryania. Dahag su dlunen tupwan ri dunya pingumpyanan mipon rin ryania.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-05.jpg}}

Dahag si Abram mengunot tu Diwata. Gewit en su sawanen si Sarai, bu sunga gulepenen muha su helunen nga bitangen dahag minangay ilan tu dlupa nga pinita nu Diwata ryanin, su dlupa Canaan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-06.jpg}}

Ba merateng na si Abram tu Canaan, metalu su Diwata, "Tinggi mu e palibot mu dlunen hin bigayen hu ren ryania bu tunga tupwan mu." Si Abram ritu na yanin megbenwa



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-07.jpg}}

One day, Abram met Melchizedek, the priest of God Most High. Melchizedek blessed Abram and said, “May God Most High who owns heaven and earth bless Abram.” Then Abram gave Melchizedek a tenth of all he owned.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-08.jpg}}

Many years went by, but Abram and Sarai still did not have a son. God spoke to Abram and promised again that he would have a son and as many descendants as the stars in the sky. Abram believed God’s promise. God declared that Abram was righteous because he believed in God’s promise.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-09.jpg}}

Then God made a covenant with Abram. A covenant is an agreement between two parties. God said, “I will give you a son from your own body. I give the land of Canaan to your descendants.” But Abram still did not have a son.
