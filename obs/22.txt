====== Su Kadlegetaw ni John ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-01.jpg}}

Su rin,  su Diwata medlegelun tu nga pedlegetawanen phebyan rin nga angel muha rin nga propheta. Ugaid mehabayan su pat gatus ton, genda na Hyanin pependa tu nga getawen. Bena su angel merating pegit thalu genat tu Diwata para tu metau pari ngalanen si Zechariah. Si Zechariah bu su sawanen nga si Elizabeth, mengunot ilan tu Diwata, ugaid daen hin bata ran.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-02.jpg}}

Su angel  theluanin si Zechariah, " Su  sawa mu merunan bata dlai. Pengalanay mu hyanin si John. Hyanin mepunu rin Mesingay Medlengaw, muha sagepaenin  su getaw para  tu gumon!" sumembag si Zechariah, "Su sama tau u (sawa u ) muha Inan  mahatau  a mena thanding para mahabata a me pa! Palas hasunan huron hini nga mehitabu?"



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-03.jpg}}

Su angel sumembag ni Zechariah, " Inan i pewet  nu Diwata para megwet ryania  hini gempya guhitin. Hagina di a mepetud ryanan, di a mahathalu sampat di mesipot su gembata." Bina, da na mahathalu si Zechariah. Dayon belingan na nu Angel  si Zechariah. Metubus itu, si  Zechariah menuli na ritu  balayen muha hitu  sawa nin megberes na.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-04.jpg}}

Sa si Elizabeth  genem bulan na hitu berissanen, su angel  nga mepeta run sadya rak mepeta tu eg agaw  ni Elizabeth, nga ngalan nin si Maria. Hyanin virgin pa muha hyanin pegbena na hin mesawanin ngalan ni si Joseph. Su angel methalu," meberissa  muha mebata a  gembata dlai. Yaa pengalanan nin mu hyanin si Jesus. Hyanin su bata nu Gempulo Balahatan  Diwata nga medlegari sampay daen getiben.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-05.jpg}}

Mary replied, “How can this be, since I am a virgin?” The angel explained, “The Holy Spirit will come to you, and the power of God will overshadow you. So the baby will be holy, the Son of God.” Mary believed and accepted what the angel said.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-06.jpg}}

Soon after the angel spoke to Mary, she went and visited Elizabeth. As soon as Elizabeth heard Mary’s greeting, Elizabeth’s baby jumped inside her. The women rejoiced together about what God had done for them. After Mary visited Elizabeth for three months, Mary returned home.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-07.jpg}}

After Elizabeth gave birth to her baby boy, Zechariah and Elizabeth named the baby John, as the angel had commanded. Then God allowed Zechariah to speak again. Zechariah said, “Praise God, because he has remembered his people! You, my son, will be called the prophet of the Most High God who will tell the people how they can receive forgiveness for their sins!”
