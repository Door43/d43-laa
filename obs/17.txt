====== Su Samaya nu Diwata rin ni David ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-01.jpg}}

Si Saul hyanin myuna gari rin Israel. Metas muha melengas dlai hyanin, sama hin su lyagan nu nga getaw nga gmebal gari. Si Saul su gempya gari shelid lak rin nda meben tun tu hepedlagari en tu Israel. Ugaid mebal hyanin balarusa nga gari muha genda en na pengunutay su Diwata. Muha su Diwata mimili dlen getaw para mebal gari sukli rin ryanin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-02.jpg}}

Pinili nu Diwata i melebud Israelite ngalanen si David puli gari rin ni Saul. Si David megingat hyanin nu nga bilibili genat tu dlungsud Bethlehem. Genda phesama dyangha sama pegingatanen su nga bilibili nu gama en, si David mehapatay dlemong muha dlupop nga human tu nga bilibili. Si David dun i helembaba en muha metidu getaw nga mephetud muha mesiligan hyanin bu mingunut tu Diwata.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-03.jpg}}

Si David mebal hyanin bantugan sundalo muha kunutan. Sa su melebud pa si David, mehuntranen su gunlu ngalanen Goliath Si Goliath mehaganad misaba nga sundalo meseheg thandeng muha pegangay na thelu metros su tasen! Ugaid gebangan hyanin nu Diwata nga mebantugen si Goliath bu mi gunen su nga Israel. Metubos itu sa, si David melun pa i mepadauganen nga buno nu nga kuntra nga Israelite, nga su nga getaw pederyal lan hyanin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-04.jpg}}

Si Saul mesegi tu ni David tu hagina su dlelaman nu getaw rin ni David. Melon dyangha nga lyagan nis Saul petayay si David, hasma maantu, megedlud hyanin tu ni Saul. Selabuk gendaw, peningaw ni Saul si David para petayenen. Menangay si Saul tu dlangob nga pegedluran rema ni David, ugaid si Saul daen meta si David. Mesempel na gupya si David tu ni Saul muha pahayen na sya petayay si Saul, ugaid daen hitu gulehay. Ugaid genuntinganen su dlaho ni Saul para  mesunanen nga gendi en hyanin petayn para hyanin mebal i  gari.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-05.jpg}}

Ugaid, minatay si Saul rin hepegbuno, dahag si David mebal gari tu Israel. hyanin su misaba gari bu lenalam hyanin tunga getaw. Su Diwata pingupyanen si David bu mibal hyanin succesful. Si David melon buno mibyanan nen bu gebangan hyanin nu Diwata nga rumaug ilan tu nga kontra lan. Si David miha sahopen su Jerusalem bu benalen eni  gebagel syudad.Sagay ni David medligari, su Israel mi bal bantogan bumerato benwa.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-06.jpg}}

Lyagan ni David megbal simbahan para nu Diwata para su dlunen getaw mehebigay hepegya tu Diwata bu mehegbigay dulang tu Diwata.  Manin patpulo gatus ton, su nga getaw sinimba lan su Diwata muha megbigay ilan nga dulang tu Diwata ritu Dlahaw Tepuhan nga benal ni Moses.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-07.jpg}}

Ugaid su Diwata peweten si propheta Nathan tu ni David i nga thalu hini, "Haguna yaa getaw tu phegbunuan,  gendi mu balay su simbahan hin para rin dyanan. Su gbata mu dlai i megbal run. Ugaid, pengumpyanan hu ra yaa thandeng. Selebok nu thupwan mu mebal gari tu pedlegetawan hu daen geteben!" Su selabok thupwan ni David nga mebal gari muha kunutan sampay daen geteben hitu i Gumon. Su Gumon, hyanin i selebok pinili nu Diwata nga gumon tu nga getaw rini dunya nga balarusa.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-08.jpg}}

Sama merengeg ni David hitu nga thalu hitu, mepashalamat  yanin bu pedelyaren su Diwata, haguna mephasad hyanin ni David gembagel bantug bu pengumpya. Genda mesunan ni David ba nanu balay nu Diwata su gbetang hitu. Ugaid, sama hetu mebal, su nga Israelite megelat metas dyanha genat su Gumon merating,  singebu ton perating.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-09.jpg}}

Si David medlegari i du patasen bu hesalegen rin melon ton, muha su Diwata pengumpyananen hyanin. Ugaid, sa metau na si David mehagbal hyanin gembagel dusa tu Diwata.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-10.jpg}}

Selabok gendaw, sa su dlunen sundalo ni David nga melayo tu nga balay lan kinpimbunuan, mitinge yanin tu dibabaw tu palasyu nen muha mehita hyanin melengas dlibon peligo. Ngalanen Bathsheba.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-11.jpg}}

Instead of looking away, David sent someone to bring her to him. He slept with her and sent her back home. A short time later Bathsheba sent a message to David saying that she was pregnant.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-12.jpg}}

Bathsheba’s husband, a man named Uriah, was one of David’s best soldiers. David called Uriah back from the battle and told him to go be with his wife. But Uriah refused to go home while the rest of the soldiers were in battle. So David sent Uriah back to the battle and told the general to place him where the enemy was strongest so that he would be killed.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-13.jpg}}

After Uriah was killed, David married Bathsheba. Later, she gave birth to David’s son. God was very angry about what David had done, so he sent the prophet Nathan to tell David how evil his sin was. David repented of his sin and God forgave him. For the rest of his life, David followed and obeyed God, even in difficult times.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-14.jpg}}

But as punishment for David’s sin, his baby boy died. There was also fighting in David’s family for the rest of his life, and David’s power was greatly weakened. Though David had been unfaithful to God, God was still faithful to his promises. Later, David and Bathsheba had another son, and they named him Solomon.
