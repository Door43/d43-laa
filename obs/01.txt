====== Su Hephegbal Din Dunya Genesis 1-2 ======

Bina methalu su Diwata, "Mebwang su herelag!" Bu mebuwang hetu kherelag. Minita nu Diwata melengas i syag  muha peningalananen "gendaw." Pedlaynin su herelag tu herelem, muha tinawagen "gebi." Benal nu Diwata su herelag ri permiro gendaw rin hegbal.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-03.jpg}}

Tu kherwaen gendaw rin hephegbal, Methalu su Diwata bina benalen su kawang ditu detas dunya. Pedlaynen su kawang phebyan ritu debabaw tubig genat tu tubig rin dlena.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-04.jpg}}

Khethelu gendawen, methalu su Diwata muha pedlaynen hitu tubig muha su dlupa. Tinawagen su memala dlupa, "dlupa," dahag su tubig tinawagen "dagat."  Minita nu Diwata nga melengas su benalen.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-05.jpg}}

Muha methalu su Diwata, " merun su gbebenyasan nga gayu muha pemula." Metwa su gbebenyasa nga gbetang.  Minita nu Diwata nga mesiba su benalen.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-06.jpg}}

Khepat gendaw tu hepegbal, su Diwata methalu yanin muha megbal gendaw, dahus bulan, biton para mahabigay herelag ri dunya bu pituusanen su gendaw bu gebi, su rema dyangha ri peris bu dupi muha ton. Minita nu Diwata alan su benalen mesheba tandeng.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-07.jpg}}

He limandaw, me talo su Diwata nga me bal su helunen nga pedlanguy tu  tubig. muha sunga pedlayug nga manok manok. Minita nu diwata alan su binalen meseba, muha pengupyanen.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-08.jpg}}

Ghenem daw en methalu su Diwata, nga metwa su helonen nga petubuen, dahus me tuwa eni. Sebad 
nga phetubuen ri gbenal, nga dlog muha minanap tu dlupa. Minita nu Diwata nga melengas gupya hetu benal en.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-09.jpg}}

Methalu su Diwata, " megbal i ta getaw sama rin ryanta para hyanin meghepat rin dlunan gbetang rini dunya."

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-10.jpg}}

Minalap Hyanin dunek dlupa muha manin palas nu getaw.  Bu bigayanen haynin gina.bu pengalananen Adam, Phegbenwa nu Diwata si Adam ritu garden para hyanin hin megepat tu dlunan gbetang rin dunya.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-11.jpg}}

Rin tetenga garden dun dwa a buk gayo pengembula nu Diwata nga da mesam tu helun gayo. Hini dwa a bok gayu hini, gayu rin hetubu "tree of life" muha gayu rin pendayan mahasun ri meseba bu  melat.  Theluan nu Diwata si Adam nga pwede nin hanay su dlunan bunga rin gayu gawas lak rin gayu rin phendayan mahasun ri meseba bu melat. Aguna, ba hanen en su gayu hitu matay hyanin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-12.jpg}}

Muha matula su Diwta,, "hena mesiba tu getaw nga sala nen lak," ugaid  deen tu nga petubuen nga mha gabang tu ni Adam.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-13.jpg}}

Su Diwata pyanehen si Adam muha minalap Hyanin rin gusok en bu peporma nen hini getaw dlebun bu gewiten tu ri ryanin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-14.jpg}}

ba minita ni Adam, bu metalu, ah henen, nga selabuk sama ryanan, bu tinwagen dlibon, haguna binal hyanin genat ri Dlai. Hyanin su phunan masap hin dlai belengan nen su gina en bu gama en aguna meselabuk ilan ritu sawa nen.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-15.jpg}}

Su Diwata benalen  su dlai bu dlibon sama rin ryanin. Pengumpyanan hilan nu Diwata muha thenaluan hilan, "Mesheklon hamo bu phipeno nyu su dunya rin nga tupwan nyu." Muha minita nu Diwata nga su dlunen benalen nga meseba  thandeng  bu lelyag thandeng tu mibalen. Henin eni i mebal rin genem daw.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-16.jpg}}

When the seventh day came, God had finished his work. So God rested from all he had been doing. He blessed the seventh day and made it holy, because on this day he rested from his work. This is how God created the universe and everything in it.
