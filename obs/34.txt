====== Menintulo Dlayn Guhetin si Jesus ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-01.jpg}}

Si Jesus meguhit pa melon guhetin  mahasud tu pedlegarian nu Diwata. Sapalati, methalu Hyanin, "Su pedlegarian nu Diwata mesama tu dliso gmustasa nga pengembula ritu gbenal. Mesunan nyu su dliso gmustasa hin i mekaka thanding nga dliso tu dlunan dliso."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-02.jpg}}

"Ugaid mba su dliso gmustasa mengentubo na, mebal gembagel thanding pengembula ritu garden, saba tu hebagel en su nga manok-manok mangay ritu bu menulali ritu nga panga  nen."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-03.jpg}}

Si Jesus meguhit dlen guhetin, "Su pedlegarian nu Diwata mesama tu selabuk patubu na ba su dlibon balen na su phan dlemugenen su patubu tu thapung sampay tumubo hini."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-04.jpg}}

"Su pedlegarian nu Diwata mesama rema tu gaus nga gidlud tu gbenal. Su dlen getaw minita en muha libengin puli. Sugat i dlelyag en muha menuli bu peshaluy en su dlunan gbetang en dahag ginamit en su pilak para sheluy en nen su gbenal."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-05.jpg}}

Su pedlegarian nu Diwata mesama rema mesiba nga gmuntya nga metas i dlaga en. Mba mesunan nu menshaluy gmuntya,  peshaluy en su dlunen gbetang en muha gemetin nen su pilak para meshaluy en itu."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-06.jpg}}

Muha si Jesus methalu dlen guhetin tu shebad nga getaw nga mephetud tu dyanlan gempya mebal lan muha...
"Dun dwa dlai menangay tu simbahan para meningi-ningi. Su selabuk hitu mengulekta buwis, dahag su selabuk hitu kunutan ri simbahan."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-07.jpg}}

"Su kunutan ri simbahan meningi-ningi sama main ni, " Shalamat Diwata, nga inan hana u balarusa da shama rin dlen getaw- nga meharaw getaw, nga dlimbungan nga getaw, bu  sama rin mengulekta buwis hin."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-08.jpg}}

“‘For example, I fast two times every week and I give you ten percent of all the money and goods that I receive.’”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-09.jpg}}

“But the tax collector stood far away from the religious leader, and did not even look up to heaven. Instead, he pounded on his chest with his fist and prayed, ‘God, please be merciful to me because I am a sinner.’”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-10.jpg}}

Then Jesus said, “I tell you the truth, God heard the tax collector’s prayer and declared him to be righteous. But he did not like the religious leader’s prayer. God will humble everyone who is proud, and he will lift up whoever humbles himself.”



// A Bible story from: Matthew 13:31-33, 44-46; Mark 4:30-32; Luke 13:18-21; 18:9-14 //
