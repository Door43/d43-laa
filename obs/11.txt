====== Exodus 11:1-12:132 ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-01.jpg}}

Su Diwata mimalinta ni Pharaoh mba di en buwangen su nga Israelite panaw, bunuenen su nga metau bata dlai nu nga Egyptian bu tu nga petubuen lan. Sama merengeg hitu ni Pharaoh genda nasap hyanin helyag nga mepetud bu mengunot tu Diwata.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-02.jpg}}

Su Diwata mebigay palas kendun i hegun tu metau bata dlai tu mengunut rin ryanin. Seselataw nu nga pamilya memeli ilan nga mesiba bata dlai bilibili bu bunuen lan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-03.jpg}}

Su Diwata theluanen su mga Israelite nga betangan lan dugo nu bata dlai bili-bili su dlumibot tu nga pinto ri balay lan, bu tetein lan su karninen dahag hanin lan dayun, perumanan lan pan nga geluto nga daerun  patubu en. Theluanen rema nga mesagipa ilan na waan su Egypt ba mahahan hilan na.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-04.jpg}}

Su mga Israelite pegulek lan su helon nga pensugu ryanlan nu Diwata nga pegbetaren lan. Dahag ba metetenga gebi na, su Diwata minangay na hyanin tu Dliwag Egypt bu pembunuen su nga metau bata dlai.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-05.jpg}}

Su dlunan balay nu nga getaw Israelite dun dugu tu nga dlumibot pinto lan, dahag su Diwata linyusanen su nga balay. Su dlunan nga shelid tu balay lan megun. Mihagun hilan mipon tu dugo nu bata dlai bilibili.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-06.jpg}}

But the Egyptians did not believe God or obey his commands. So God did not pass over their houses. God killed every one of the Egyptians’ firstborn sons.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-07.jpg}}

Every Egyptian firstborn male died, from the firstborn of the prisoner in jail, to the firstborn of Pharaoh. Many people in Egypt were crying and wailing because of their deep sadness.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-08.jpg}}

That same night, Pharaoh called for Moses and Aaron and said, “Take the Israelites and leave Egypt immediately!” The Egyptian people also urged the Israelites to leave immediately.
